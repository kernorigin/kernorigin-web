Why We Do This

Most consultants sell solutions. We find root causes. Why?

Because we've watched too many organizations waste money on band-aid solutions that don't stick. We've seen leadership teams exhaust themselves working harder when they should be working differently. We've seen potential squandered because the real problem was never diagnosed.

That's wasteful. And we don't do wasteful work.
