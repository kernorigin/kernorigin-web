Who Partners With Us

The right partner already serves the clients we want to reach.

You do not need to be a consulting firm to be a Kernorigin partner. You need to have a trust relationship with business owners and leaders — and want to deepen that relationship with a diagnostic product that actually works.

CA & CS Firms

You see the financial symptoms. The diagnostic finds the root cause. Offer your business clients a 35-page root cause report co-branded with your firm — and become the advisor who found what the accounts couldn't show.

PE & VC Firms

Run institutional diagnostics on portfolio companies before and after investment decisions. A co-branded Kernorigin diagnostic is a credibility-enhancing due diligence tool that gives you a structured view of organisational root causes.

Incubators & Accelerators

Your cohorts are exactly the right profile — growth-stage businesses with operational dysfunction they haven't yet diagnosed. Make root cause diagnostics a structured part of your programme curriculum.

HR & OD Firms

You see the People and Leadership cylinders clearly. Kernorigin completes the picture — you can offer clients a diagnostic that maps all seven cylinders, not just the human dimension. Stronger mandate, deeper engagement.

Bank MSME Desks

MSME and SME advisory programmes need structured diagnostic tools to offer clients alongside credit products. Kernorigin diagnostics integrate naturally — they identify the root cause of why a business is underperforming before a loan can help it.

International Advisors

Business advisory firms in Dubai, Singapore, and the UK serving Indian diaspora businesses and South Asian markets. Diagnostics are available in English, Hindi, and Kannada — with USD, AED, and GBP pricing for international clients.

What You Get

Four things every partner receives.

01

A co-branded diagnostic product

Every diagnostic report sent to your clients carries your firm's name alongside Kernorigin's. Your brand in the header, your contact details in the footer. A 35-page or 50-page report your clients will read and reference — with your name on it.

02

Revenue share on every diagnostic

25% of every paid diagnostic your clients complete. That is ₹6,249 per Institutional Diagnostic and ₹12,499 per Systems Diagnostic. Paid monthly, automatically, with a full transaction report. No invoice required from your side.

03

Your own partner landing page

A unique URL branded to your firm — kernorigin.com/partners/[yourfirm] — where your clients can take the free assessment or purchase a paid diagnostic directly. Track completions and conversions from your partner dashboard.

04

Partner support & enablement

A 30-minute orientation call with Peria F. Malayappa on how to position the diagnostic to your clients. A batch of 10 complimentary coupon codes for your first client introductions. A partner kit with talking points, FAQ, and pricing materials.

Revenue share scales with volume. Partners completing 20+ diagnostics per month qualify for an enhanced share rate. Partners who co-sell the advisory program receive additional commission. Active partners who refer other partner firms to the ecosystem receive a referral bonus on the first 6 months of that partner's activity.
