Case Studies

Engineering College

From 40% to 72% Placements in One Year

The Symptom: Declining placement rates. Good employers stopped coming. Average salaries dropping year over year.

What Everyone Blamed: "The economy is bad." "Employers are too picky." "Curriculum needs updating."

The Root Cause: No diagnostic system to assess student readiness. The college was sending unprepared students to interviews, damaging relationships with employers.

The Fix: Built a comprehensive student readiness diagnostic. Segmented students into three tiers: Employable Now, Trainable, and Foundation Needed. Provided targeted interventions for each tier instead of generic training for all.

The Result:

  • Placements increased from 40% to 72% in 12 months
  • Average salary increased 1.2X
  • Top-tier employers returned to campus
  • Training ROI improved 3X (focused intervention vs. generic)
B2B Services Firm

Breaking Through ₹50Cr Revenue Ceiling

The Symptom: Revenue stuck at ₹45-50Cr for 3 years. Leadership working harder than ever. Team burned out.

What Everyone Blamed: "Market is saturated." "We need better sales." "Competition is aggressive."

The Root Cause: Operations couldn't scale. Every new client required heroics to deliver. Manual processes that worked at ₹20Cr were breaking at ₹50Cr.

The Fix: Rebuilt operational systems from scratch. Automated critical workflows. Created scalable delivery processes. Built capability in the team to manage higher complexity.

The Result:

  • Revenue grew from ₹50Cr to ₹78Cr in 18 months
  • Delivery capacity increased 2.5X without adding headcount
  • Client satisfaction improved (measured via NPS: 42 → 68)
  • Leadership stopped firefighting, started strategizing
Manufacturing Unit

Eliminating ₹2.3Cr Annual Cash Leakage

The Symptom: Margins shrinking despite steady revenue. Working capital always tight. Can't understand where money is going.

What Everyone Blamed: "Raw material costs are rising." "Labor costs are up." "We need to cut expenses."

The Root Cause: Inventory was sitting too long (inventory turnover < 4X). Cash was trapped in slow-moving stock. No system to identify or liquidate dead inventory.

The Fix: Implemented ABC analysis of inventory. Created just-in-time procurement protocols. Built a monthly cash flow diagnostic system.

The Result:

  • Freed up ₹2.3Cr in trapped working capital
  • Inventory turnover improved from 3.8X to 6.2X
  • Cash flow visibility increased (real-time dashboards)
  • Margins recovered without cutting headcount or quality

The Pattern Is Clear

Leadership thinks the problem is external. We diagnose internal dysfunction. We fix the root cause. Growth returns.
