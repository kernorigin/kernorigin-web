Our Process

We Don't Just Recommend.
We Implement.

From a 2-day workshop to an 18-month transformation, our process stays the same: diagnose root causes, design solutions that fit your context, deliver results, and sustain change.

The Universal Process

Whether you engage us for ₹50K or ₹50L, this is how we work.

1

DIAGNOSE

We don't assume. We investigate.

Most organizations know the symptom (low sales, high turnover, operational chaos) but not the root cause. We use the Seven Cylinders Framework™ to separate what you're experiencing from what's actually broken.

What This Looks Like:

  • Stakeholder interviews
  • Process mapping
  • Data analysis
  • System audits
  • Root cause identification
2

DESIGN

We don't copy/paste. We custom-build.

We've seen 50+ organizations. No two solutions have been identical. We design interventions that fit YOUR context, constraints, culture, and capacity. What worked for Company A won't work for Company B.

What This Looks Like:

  • Custom roadmaps (not templates)
  • Phased implementation plans
  • Resource allocation
  • Risk mitigation strategies
  • Success metrics definition
3

DELIVER

We don't just recommend. We implement.

This is where most consultants stop. They give you a report and leave. We stay. We implement. We work alongside your team until the solution is working, not just documented.

What This Looks Like:

  • Hands-on implementation support
  • Team training and coaching
  • Process optimization in real-time
  • Course corrections as needed
  • Measurable results tracking
4

SUSTAIN (Optional)

We don't abandon. We partner.

Change doesn't stick automatically. Old habits return. New problems emerge. For clients who want ongoing support, we offer retained partnerships. Not required, but available.

What This Looks Like:

  • Quarterly strategic sessions
  • Continuous improvement cycles
  • On-demand diagnostic support
  • Leadership coaching
  • Long-term strategic partnership
