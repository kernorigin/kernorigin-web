Root Cause Thinking

Everyone sees the symptom.
No one finds the root cause.

The Seven Cylinders

Every organization runs on seven cylinders. When growth stalls, 1-3 cylinders are failing.

  • 01 STRATEGY: Where are you actually going?
  • 02 LEADERSHIP: Who's driving? Are they aligned?
  • 03 OPERATIONS: How does work actually get done?
  • 04 PEOPLE: Right people? Right roles?
  • 05 SYSTEMS: What enables progress? What blocks it?
  • 06 CULTURE: What behaviors are actually rewarded?
  • 07 CUSTOMER: Who are you serving? Why do they choose you?

AI + Human Intelligence

Root causes hide in patterns AND context.

AI finds patterns humans miss:
Financial anomalies, Operational bottlenecks, Performance drift.

Humans find context AI can't:
Why people say one thing and do another. Political dynamics.

We combine both. That's how we find what's actually broken.
